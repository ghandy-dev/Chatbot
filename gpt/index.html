Usage
Conversations are based on the context of the channel, and Gpt maintains a history of messages sent by chatters for up to 10 minutes from the last message sent. i.e. 10 mins after sending no messages through the gpt command will wipe your chatting history with it > gpt <prompt> > gpt What are the some of the rarest deep sea creatures? Use a specific persona that affect how GPT interprets the conversation, and its own role in the conversation. Currently has: - default - Default model used. GPT is a helpful assistant. - bully - GPT is mocking and belittling in hopefully a light-hearted manner. > gpt <persona> <prompt>
